How Requity helps you find a real estate agent match

Requity is a real estate agent matching and relationship platform. Instead of handing you a directory, Requity starts by understanding you: your communication style, your decision making, and what your move requires. Here is the client agent matching process from start to finish.

The four steps

1. Tell us what you are looking for

Share whether you are buying, selling, or both, along with your market and contact details.

2. Complete a short relationship style assessment

Answer questions about how you communicate, make decisions, and handle stress. This is the real estate personality assessment at the core of Requity.

3. Requity reviews your profile

A real Requity team member reviews your answers, communication style, and home goals. Typical review time is 24 to 48 hours.

4. Get matched with an agent whose style fits your goals

Requity considers personality insights, transaction needs, and location, then notifies you when your agent match is ready.

What makes the matching meaningful

Agents on Requity complete their own assessment and receive an agent archetype that describes their working and communication style. That means matching is based on insight from both sides of the relationship, not just a client questionnaire. Read more about real estate agent matching, or see the guidance for buyers and sellers.

Frequently asked questions

How does agent matching work on Requity?

You tell Requity what you are looking for, complete a short relationship style assessment, and a Requity team member reviews your profile. Requity then supports a match with an agent whose communication style and coverage fit your goals.

What is a real estate personality assessment?

It is a short set of questions about how you communicate, make decisions, and handle stress during a transaction. Requity uses these insights to understand what kind of agent relationship may fit you.

How long does the Requity review take?

A typical review takes 24 to 48 hours. You are notified when your match is ready.

Is Requity free for buyers and sellers?

Starting with the client assessment is free for buyers and sellers.
