Frequently asked questions

How does Requity help me find a real estate agent?

Requity starts with a short assessment that helps us understand how you communicate, what kind of guidance you want, and what you need from a buying or selling process. A real person reviews the match before any connection is made, so the fit is considered with care.

Is Requity just another lead form?

No. Requity starts with a short assessment so we can better understand your goals, market, communication preferences, and the type of guidance you want. Matches are reviewed before connection so the process is more thoughtful than a simple lead form.

Do I have to commit to an agent right away?

No. Requity is free to start. The goal is to help you make a more informed connection, not pressure you into a decision before you are ready.

How does Requity choose a real estate agent?

Requity looks at your assessment, your market, and the type of support you want. When a match is reviewed, we consider communication style, working approach, and fit for your buying or selling needs.

Is my information used for matching?

Yes. The information you share helps Requity understand your needs and support the matching process.

Why does communication style matter?

Buying or selling can involve timing, pressure, and important decisions. Working with an agent whose communication style fits yours can make the process feel clearer and easier to manage.

Is Requity for buyers or sellers?

Requity is designed for buyers, sellers, and people who are both buying and selling. The assessment adapts based on what you are trying to do.

What makes Requity different from a normal agent directory?

Requity is different from a simple agent directory because it combines a client assessment, a reviewed matching process, and a brokerage and referral workflow. It focuses on communication style, working style, transaction needs, and market fit between clients and agents.

Can real estate agents use Requity?

Yes. Agents can complete an agent assessment, receive an agent archetype, and use Requity to better understand client communication styles and relationship needs.

Is REQUITY a real estate brokerage?

Yes. REQUITY operates as a real estate brokerage and referral based matching platform. REQUITY helps buyers and sellers complete an assessment, reviews their needs and market, and connects them with agents in the appropriate market. When a referred transaction closes, REQUITY receives a referral fee according to the applicable agent or broker agreement.

What happens if I am buying and selling?

If you are buying and selling, REQUITY may review each side of the move separately. You may be matched with an agent in the market where you want to buy, an agent in the market where you want to sell, or one agent if the same agent is appropriate for both sides.
