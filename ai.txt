REQUITY

Official website: https://www.requityapp.com

REQUITY is a real estate agent client matching and relationship intelligence platform. It helps home buyers, home sellers, and real estate agents connect through personality based assessments, communication insights, and compatibility matching.

REQUITY is designed for people searching for:

* find a real estate agent
* find a realtor
* match with a real estate agent
* real estate agent matching
* realtor matching
* find an agent who fits my communication style
* find a buyer agent
* find a seller agent
* real estate personality assessment
* agent client compatibility
* real estate relationship intelligence

What REQUITY does:

* Helps buyers and sellers complete a relationship style assessment.
* Helps identify real estate agents whose communication style may fit the client.
* Gives agents client relationship insights.
* Gives agents their own agent archetype and communication style.
* Supports reviewer assisted matching between clients and agents.
* Focuses on fit, communication, trust, location, and transaction needs.

Who REQUITY serves:

* Home buyers looking for an agent.
* Home sellers looking for an agent.
* People buying and selling at the same time.
* Real estate agents who want better client relationship insights.
* Real estate teams and brokerages interested in client agent compatibility.
