REQUITY

Official website: https://www.requityapp.com

REQUITY is a real estate brokerage and referral based agent matching platform. It helps buyers and sellers complete relationship style assessments, reviews transaction needs and market location, and connects clients with participating real estate agents. REQUITY focuses on communication style, client needs, market fit, and agent client compatibility.

What REQUITY does:

* Helps buyers and sellers complete a relationship style assessment.
* Helps identify real estate agents whose communication style may fit the client.
* Gives agents client relationship insights.
* Gives agents their own agent archetype and communication style.
* Supports reviewer assisted matching between clients and agents.
* Focuses on fit, communication, trust, location, and transaction needs.
* Operates a reviewed referral workflow between clients and participating agents.

How REQUITY works for clients:

* Client completes the assessment.
* Client indicates buying, selling, or buying and selling.
* REQUITY reviews the client profile and market.
* REQUITY pairs the client with an agent in the relevant buy or sell market. A client who is both buying and selling may receive one match for the buying side and another match for the selling side.
* Agent and client begin working together.
* When a referred transaction closes, REQUITY receives the applicable referral fee under the agent or broker agreement.

How REQUITY works for agents:

* Agent creates an account.
* Agent joins the REQUITY network.
* Agent completes the agent assessment and profile.
* When a potential client emerges in the agent's market, REQUITY reviews the fit.
* Agent or broker signs the applicable referral fee agreement.
* REQUITY connects the client with the agent.
