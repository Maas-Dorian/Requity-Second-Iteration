Find a listing agent who fits your selling goals

Selling a home requires trust, timing, and clear communication. Your listing agent guides pricing, preparation, marketing, offers, and negotiation. The right seller agent also communicates strategy in a way that fits you, whether you want direct recommendations or a more collaborative approach.

What to look for in a listing agent

  • Local market knowledge and a clear pricing approach
  • A concrete plan for preparation, staging, and marketing
  • Straightforward communication about offers and negotiation
  • Responsiveness during showings and the offer window
  • A working style that fits how you make decisions

Frequently asked questions

How do I find a listing agent to sell my house?

You can find a listing agent through referrals, local brokerages, directories, and agent matching platforms like Requity that consider your selling goals and communication style.

How do I choose a listing agent?

Look at local market knowledge, pricing approach, marketing plan, communication style, and how clearly the agent explains their strategy for preparation, offers, and negotiation.

What if I am selling and buying at the same time?

The Requity assessment adapts when you are both selling and buying, and considers whether an agent can support both sides of your move, including location coverage for each market.

Does Requity guarantee a top listing agent?

No. Requity does not guarantee outcomes. Requity uses assessments, communication insights, and human review to support a listing agent fit that matches your selling goals.
