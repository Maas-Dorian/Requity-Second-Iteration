How REQUITY Works

Getting started is simple. Agents begin with a professional style assessment to understand how they naturally communicate and guide clients. Clients complete a short relationship-based real estate assessment that identifies their preferences, motivations, communication style, and buying or selling needs. REQUITY then turns those insights into clear reports and compatibility guidance so agents and clients connect more effectively. Agents who join the REQUITY network can also receive potential client referrals in their market: when a fit is reviewed, the agent or broker signs the applicable referral fee agreement before REQUITY connects the client with the agent.
