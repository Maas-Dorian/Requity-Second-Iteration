Where to find good real estate agents

You can find good real estate agents through referrals, local directories, brokerage websites, open houses, real estate marketplaces, and agent matching platforms. The best choice depends on whether you want a simple list of agents or a more guided way to find someone whose communication style, experience, and working approach fit your needs.

Common ways to find a real estate agent

  • Personal referrals from people who recently bought or sold
  • Local brokerage websites
  • Open houses
  • Realtor directories
  • Real estate marketplaces
  • Agent matching platforms
  • Relationship based matching through Requity

Traditional directories often show a list of agents. Requity focuses on helping clients understand what kind of agent relationship may fit them best, whether you are buying a home or selling one.

Why fit matters when choosing an agent

A strong real estate agent is not only experienced. The right agent should also communicate in a way that helps you feel informed, supported, and confident. Some buyers want detailed explanations. Some sellers want direct strategy. Some clients need calm guidance during stressful moments. Requity focuses on this relationship fit.

How Requity helps

Requity helps buyers and sellers complete a short relationship style assessment. The assessment helps identify what kind of support, communication, and guidance a client may need from a real estate agent. Requity then supports agent matching based on personality insights, transaction needs, and location. You can learn more about the process on the how it works page, or read about real estate agent matching in more detail.

Frequently asked questions

Where can I find good real estate agents?

You can find good real estate agents through referrals, local brokerages, Realtor directories, real estate marketplaces, open houses, and agent matching platforms like Requity.

What should I look for in a real estate agent?

Look for local experience, communication style, responsiveness, trust, transaction knowledge, and whether the agent's working style fits how you make decisions.

Is Requity an agent directory?

Requity is not just a directory. Requity focuses on relationship fit, communication style, client needs, and compatibility between clients and agents.

Can Requity help buyers and sellers?

Yes. Requity is designed for buyers, sellers, and people who are buying and selling at the same time.
