Real estate agent matching based on communication and fit

Real estate agent matching is a guided alternative to browsing agent directories. Instead of starting with a long list of names, matching starts with you: how you communicate, how you make decisions, what kind of support you want, and what your transaction requires. Requity is an agent matching platform built around this idea of agent client compatibility.

What goes into a good match

  • Communication style: how often and how directly you want updates and explanations
  • Personality fit: how you handle decisions, stress, and negotiation moments
  • Transaction needs: buying, selling, or both at the same time
  • Location: an agent who actually serves your market
  • Support level: hands-on guidance or a lighter touch

Traditional directories often show a list of agents. Requity focuses on helping clients understand what kind of agent relationship may fit them best. If you are just starting, see where to find good real estate agents for an overview of all your options.

How Requity approaches matching

Requity uses a relationship style assessment to understand how you communicate, what support you need, and what kind of real estate experience you are looking for. Agents on Requity complete their own assessment and receive an agent archetype that describes their working and communication style. A Requity reviewer then considers personality insights, transaction needs, and location before a match is finalized. Learn more on the how it works page.

Matching supports buyers and sellers differently. Buyers can read about finding a buyer agent, and sellers can read about finding a listing agent.

Frequently asked questions

What is real estate agent matching?

Real estate agent matching is a way to connect buyers and sellers with agents based on fit rather than only browsing a list. Matching can consider communication style, personality, transaction needs, and location.

What is agent client compatibility?

Agent client compatibility describes how well an agent's communication style, pace, and working approach fit a client's preferences and needs during a real estate transaction.

How does Requity match clients with agents?

Requity uses a relationship style assessment to understand how a client communicates and what support they need. Requity then supports matching based on personality insights, transaction needs, and location, with human review before a match is finalized.

Is agent matching better than a directory?

Directories and matching serve different needs. A directory shows many agents to browse. Matching focuses on helping you understand which kind of agent relationship may fit you best.
